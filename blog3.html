Personal SWOT analysis

Strengths

I am pretty good at subjects that are analytical and have abstract problems, like Maths (variant B, “Wiskunde-B”) and Physics. This can help me with programming because I'll more easily find problems and how to fix them.

Weaknesses

I am quite shy, which makes it hard for me to communicate and/or work in a team. I am also inclined to procrastinate (though not the kind of procrastinating where you have to create an entire website in one evening for example), which is of course not a good thing. Combined with that procrastinating is also a little bit of perfectionism, which complicates things.

Opportunities

Gaining experience with working in teams, which will come in handy at an actual IT job. The projects you’ll do for real companies and the internship you’ll follow as part of your study will make you known to companies in the area (networking) and might enable you to find jobs more easily.

Threats

I currently don’t see any threats.
