Programming experience

Programming experience prior to study

The only prior experience I had with programming that I didn’t give up/ forgot after 2 days is modding games, to be more specific modding the game “Hearts of Iron IV”. I started doing this in the summer vacation, partly to prepare for the study “HBO-ICT” (to gain just a little bit of experience in programming).

Hearts of iron 4 modding

The first 2 mods I made were small and very specific, having influence on everything in the game without any selectors. The next 2 mods were bug fixes of bugs in the base game. I encountered those bugs working on my biggest mod, namely a mod that multiplies all modifiers in the game by 10. This wasn’t an original idea: there were multiple other mods that did the same thing (multiply by 10, multiply by 1000), but those were all outdated and not compatible with the most recent version of the game. I enjoyed playing those mods when they were still compatible, so I took it upon myself to make an updated version. Right now I’m about 95% done with it, only the “events” (popups with effects/ information) need to be done, but I’ve stopped working on it for quite a while because of school/ spending my free time on other things. I kept a log on the amount of time I spent working on it, which currently totals out at around 120 hours.
