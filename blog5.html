First feedback

Feedback (Translated from Dutch)

“Welcome to the study HBO-ICT. Maths will indeed help you with programming, it’s good that you’ve chosen ICT for your interest in soft- and hardware. A lot of students have an interest in gaming. We do have a wide education so apart from making an educational game in quartile 2 of year 1, you’ll also learn a lot of other things. Keep in mind that there will be a lot of work done in groups onwards from quartile 2 (in year 1) and that we spend a lot of attention on personal development and getting to know your teammates.”

My thoughts

It’s nice to hear that maths (and other more abstract subjects) will help me with programming. I also actually quite like the fact that it’s a pretty wide education, because that will make me versatile and able to choose from a large variety of IT roles at companies. It’s also reassuring to read that they spend a lot of attention on personal development and getting to know people.
