Study Choice

Study choice activities

I learnt about the study “HBO-ICT” for the first time in the HZ Vlissingen (on 15-11-2019) when I and the other students in my class had a kind of “open day” where you could go and learn about 3 studies in total, I chose HBO-ICT as one of my three options. I didn’t really know whether this was the study for me, but I still decided to go to another “open day” at the HZ in Middelburg on 27-11-2021. After hearing more about the study there, I decided that I liked the study and I signed up for it.

Motivate your study choice

I always knew I wanted to do something with computers and programming, but I didn’t know what exactly until visiting the “open day” on 27-11-2021. Before that, I was mostly interested in game development, because at that time most of my free time was spent on gaming. It’s still a dream of mine now to one day develop my whole own game, but I know the game development industry is quite toxic and it can be hard to find a job there. Moreover, I’d like to be more versatile than just know how to create games. That’s part of the reason I chose this study. Other things I liked was the game development in quartile 2, the half year in year 3 (I believe) where you can do part of another study, accessibility (from Arnemuiden) and it was recommended a lot when searching online.

After completing the study

I think I already know which of the 3 options of specialisation I’m going for: software engineer. I like to create things and watch the things I change reflect in the software. Another plus is that this probably has the most overlap with game development out of the three specialisations, which will make it easier for me to develop my own game.
